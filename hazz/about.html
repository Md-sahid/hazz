About Us

Welcome to Al Hera International Travels

Welcome to Al Hera International Travels, your trusted partner for an exceptional Umrah pilgrimage experience. Founded by Abu Talha Khan in 2015, our agency specializes exclusively in facilitating Umrah journeys, ensuring that every pilgrim receives the utmost care and support throughout their travel experience. With over a decade of experience in the industry, we have earned a reputation for excellence and reliability among our clients.

Our Mission

At Al Hera International Travels, our mission is simple yet profound: to make the sacred journey of Umrah accessible, meaningful, and unforgettable for every Muslim. We understand the significance of this pilgrimage and are dedicated to helping you connect with your faith in a profound way. Our aim is to provide you with a seamless travel experience, allowing you to focus on your spiritual journey without the stress of logistics.
